# catio

An interactive 3D model of our V1 catio — a modular, wire-panel cat enclosure
tucked into the corner of the back deck. Reconstructed from photos and tape
measurements, one correction round at a time, with Claude.

## View it

Open `index.html` in any browser (no build, no dependencies beyond a Three.js
CDN). Or turn on GitHub Pages (Settings → Pages → deploy from `main` / root)
and it'll be live at `https://mhotwagner.github.io/catio/`.

## Controls

- **drag** to orbit, **scroll / pinch** to zoom, **shift-drag** to pan
- **SE corner / East face / South end / Top** — camera presets
- **Hide wire** — see through the welded-wire panels
- **Hide roof** — lift the shed roof (plus header, gutter, shingles) off
- **Explode** — pull the four modular panels apart to show the pegged,
  doweled construction
- **Open door / Open flap** — swing the top-hinged access door and the
  window cat flap
- **Dimensions** — measurement callouts, plus the residents' name tags

## The build (V1, as measured)

- Front (south) panel 38" × 80" with a top-hinged door over the middle section
- Two east panels, 39" × 80" each; panel sections 22.5" / 29" / 22.5" between
  2×2 rails; quadrilateral top panel 30" (N) → 6" (S) under the shed roofline
- All frames 2×2 pine, half-lap + peg joins, stained and sealed; panels are
  individually built and doweled together so the whole thing knocks down flat
- Two full-panel-width platforms mounted on the rail dividers (no walkway —
  they leap it)

## Residents

Zoey (up top) and Hoby (down low).
