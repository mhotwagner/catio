# catio

Interactive 3D models of our catio — a modular, wire-panel cat enclosure
tucked into the corner of the back deck. Reconstructed from photos and tape
measurements, one correction round at a time, with Claude.

## The models

- **V1 — as built** → [`index.html`](index.html) (measured & verified)
- **V2 — expansion draft** → [`v2.html`](v2.html) (in progress)

## Controls

Shared: drag to orbit, scroll/pinch to zoom, shift-drag to pan; camera
presets; **Hide wire**; **Hide roof**; **Explode** (the panels really do come
apart — it's all pegged); **Open door** / **Open flap**; **Dimensions**
(measurement callouts + resident name tags).

V2 adds: **Hide TBD ceiling**, **Door: W corner / middle**, and
**Show V1 interior** (the old setup, stowed for reference).

## V1 (as measured)

- Front (south) panel 38" × 80" with a top-hinged door over the middle section
- Two east panels, 39" × 80" each; panel sections 22.5" / 29" / 22.5" between
  2×2 rails; quadrilateral top panel 30" (N) → 6" (S) under the shed roofline
- All frames 2×2 pine, half-lap + peg joins, stained and sealed; panels are
  individually built and doweled together so the whole thing knocks down flat
- Two full-panel-width platforms mounted on the rail dividers (no walkway —
  they leap it)

## V2 (the plan so far)

- East panels pulled out to the house-door frame: footprint grows from
  38" to 88" wide (× 78" long) — roughly 2.3× the floor
- New south run, **locked**: 26" full-height door panel + 24" wire panel in
  yellow pine, with V1's 38" panel sliding to the east corner
- Door position still open — W corner vs middle; toggle it in the model
- Outdoor sectional along the **full north wall** plus a short chaise on the
  west wall: the window cat-flap exits directly onto the sofa back.
  This is the point of the entire project.
- Apparatus wall drafted (east + SE corner): staggered cedar shelf runs
  with a 20° cleated senior ramp — every rise on the senior spine ≤ 10¼",
  a faster three-hop lane up the north bay, and a corner survey plate +
  top run riding the 55¼" rail datum (23" headroom under the TBD ceiling)
- Phase 1 complete: a winter cubby (20×20×16" interior, 7" south-facing
  door, raised floor, shed roof) sits as a freestanding island — its roof
  is a route node between the sofa seat and the stair treads
- Roof: unresolved. Leading candidate is a flat wire ceiling at 80",
  rendered amber in the model until it earns a real color.
- Next up: the roof call (it now gates the skywalk perimeter and the
  branch-tree top anchor), then Phase 2 — skywalk, branch tree, stumps
